PK

A quiet conviction
for capital markets.

Finance, for me, was never just about numbers — it was always about understanding what those numbers mean for the people and businesses behind them.

Growing up with a deep curiosity for how value is created, discovered, and communicated, I gravitated naturally towards investor relations and capital markets. The intersection of data, narrative, and decision-making is where I feel most alive professionally.

Through the CIBOP program at Imarticus Learning, I built a rigorous foundation in financial analysis, market research, and investor communication — translating complex data into narratives that move capital.

I'm pursuing the CFA designation not just as a credential, but as a commitment — to the rigour, ethics, and depth of thinking that the best investors embody. I believe in building slowly, intentionally, and with long-term conviction.
